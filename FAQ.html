Frequently Asked Questions

What is DShare? ▼
DShare is a secure online platform that allows you to store, manage, and share your personal and government-issued documents electronically, safely, and conveniently.
How do I upload documents to DShare? ▼
To upload documents, log in to your DShare account, navigate to the "Upload Document" section, select the type of document you want to upload, and then choose the file from your device to upload securely.
Is my data secure on DShare? ▼
Yes, DShare employs advanced encryption and data protection protocols to ensure your documents and personal information are stored securely and only accessible by authorized users.
Can I share documents with others? ▼
Absolutely! DShare allows you to share your documents with trusted parties through a secure link. You can manage permissions and control access at any time.
What types of documents can I store on DShare? ▼
You can store a variety of documents, including identification documents (like Aadhaar, PAN), educational certificates, financial documents, health records, and more.
How do I reset my password? ▼
If you've forgotten your password, go to the login page, click on "Forgot Password," and follow the instructions to reset it. You’ll need access to your registered email or phone for verification.
